Like Streaks, but for money.

Streaks (For YNAB) is an iOS app that helps you build and maintain better spending habits by analyzing your YNAB transactions and tracking your progress over time.

🎯 What are streaks?

Streaks is a way to track habits via a "Don't Break The Chain" methodology. The purpose is to gamify your spending habits so that you feel more motivated to avoid wasteful spending and engage in meaningful spending and saving.

✨ Key Features

📊 Gamifying Spending Habits

  • Define custom spending habits based on your YNAB categories
  • Track your progress with visual streak counters
  • Set spending limits and monitor your adherence

🔒 Privacy-First Design

Your privacy is our top priority.

  • All data processing happens locally on your device
  • Uses secure OAuth 2.0 authentication with YNAB
  • Stores sensitive tokens in iOS Keychain
  • No analytics or tracking services

Read our Privacy Policy.

📱 Native iOS Experience

  • Designed specifically for iOS with modern UI/UX
  • Dark mode support
  • Auto-sync when app becomes active

🚀 How It Works

  1. Connect to YNAB - Securely link your YNAB account
  2. Define Habits - Create spending habits based on your categories
  3. Track Progress - Monitor your streaks and spending patterns
  4. Build Better Habits - Use insights to improve your financial behavior

🛠️ Technical Details

  • Platform: iOS 18.5+
  • Authentication: OAuth 2.0 Implicit Grant Flow
  • Data Storage: Local device storage only
  • API Integration: YNAB API v1
  • Security: HTTPS with TLS 1.2+ and App Transport Security
